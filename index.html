How to play
Roles

Two roles, one secret

CitizenSees the secret word — describe it without giving it away
ImpostorNo word — just a vague hint if hints are on. Blend in and survive.
Each round

Talk, vote, eliminate

Each player sees their card privately
Everyone says one word to describe their word
Tap the players you suspect to vote
Confirm — most votes gets eliminated
Repeat until all impostors are found — or they outnumber citizens
Last impostor: if they guess the citizen word — they win
