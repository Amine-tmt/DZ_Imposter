How to play
01 — Roles

Three roles, one truth

CitizenSees the secret word — describe it without giving it away
ImpostorNo word — just a vague hint. Bluff and survive.
BlankNothing at all — listen, guess, don't get caught
02 — Each Round

Talk, vote, eliminate

Pass the phone — each player peeks their card privately
Going around, each player says one word to describe their word
Discuss — who seems sus?
Vote — most votes gets eliminated
Last impostor: if they guess the citizen word — they still win
03 — Hints

The hint system

The impostor has no word. With hints ON, they see one vague clue to help them bluff. With hints OFF — total darkness. Citizens never see hints.
